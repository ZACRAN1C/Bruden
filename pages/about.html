A brand of small products that make a huge difference to your everyday look

We strive to produce quality fashionable products that adhere to your comfort. We currently have 15 products in-store and more to come!

We will be the brand that produces small products that make a huge difference in your day-to-day outfits. Having that fashionable yet still comfortable piece to your fit can make a big impact on your day-to-day life.

  • Unique size for maximum comfort

    Our backpacks are not normal-sized. They are made in a mini size since this really is an alternative to a handbag, not solely in size but also in how you wear it.

  • A Healthy back and straight posture

    Two shoulder straps distribute the weight more evenly across the back. This will make women feel more comfortable while walking and reduces the likelihood of muscle spasms and poor posture

  • An alternative to carrying a handbag

    Our number one product, Bruden's Backpack was created for those who need an alternative to wearing a handbag. Those who prefer to wear their bags on their back than on just one shoulder
